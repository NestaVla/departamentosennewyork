Resolvemos tus dudas

What is the minimum stay?
Most apartments require a 5-night minimum. Some units accept 3–4 night stays depending on availability. Ask us via WhatsApp and we'll check for your specific dates.
How do I book without Airbnb?
Send us a WhatsApp message with your dates, number of guests and preferred area. We'll reply with available options and a price. Once you confirm, we'll send payment instructions.
Are the apartments safe and clean?
Yes. All units are personally verified by our team, professionally cleaned between guests, and located in safe, well-connected Manhattan neighborhoods.
Can I get a receipt or invoice?
Yes, we can provide a payment confirmation and receipt for all bookings. Let us know if you need it for business expenses.
What if something goes wrong during my stay?
You have our WhatsApp number for the duration of your stay. We respond within minutes — much faster than any Airbnb or hotel support team.
